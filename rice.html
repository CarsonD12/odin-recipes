Breakfast Mixed Rice

This recipe is good for a quick morning breakfast and has about 20 grams of protien!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • cup of rice pre cooked
  • about half a cup of frozen vegetables
  • a spoonful of butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. heat up pan and let butter melt
  2. add vegetables and eggs and cook until scrambled
  3. mix in the rice, salt, and pepper
  4. enjoy!
